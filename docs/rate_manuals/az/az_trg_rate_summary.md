## Hold-Open Policy Structure (Section 109)

**Applies to**: Licensed real estate brokers/agents, licensed contractors, and employers under employee transfer programs purchasing land with intent to sell to a third party within 2 years.

### Initial Purchase (First Acquisition)
- **Premium**: Applicable policy type rate (100%, 150%, or 110% of Basic Rate)
- **Hold-Open Fee**: Additional 25% of the applicable policy type rate
- **Minimum Hold-Open Fee**: $250

### Resale to Ultimate Purchaser (within 2 years)
- **Premium**: Applicable policy type rate for the new purchase price
- **Credit**: Minus the premium paid on the initial purchase (NOT including the 25% hold-open fee)
- **Net Premium**: New premium minus credit

**Example**:
```
First Acquisition (Opendoor purchase):
- Liability: $300,000
- Policy Type: Homeowner's (110% of Basic Rate)
- Premium: $1,515
- Hold-Open Fee (25%): $379
- Total Paid: $1,894

Ultimate Purchaser (Opendoor sale):
- Liability: $400,000
- Policy Type: Homeowner's (110% of Basic Rate)
- Premium: $1,780
- Credit: -$1,515 (initial premium only, not the $379 hold-open fee)
- Net Premium Due: $265
```

## Loan Policies - With Concurrent Full Value Owner's Policy (Section 202)

When issued concurrently with an owner's policy for full fair value covering the same land:

| Lender Policy Type | Owner Policy Type | Charge |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Coverage | Standard Coverage or Homeowner's | $100 flat fee |
| Extended Coverage (Region 1) | Standard Coverage or Homeowner's | 70% of Basic Rate (Min: $730) |
| Extended Coverage (Region 2) | Standard Coverage or Homeowner's | 65% of Basic Rate (Min: $600) |
| Extended Coverage | Extended Coverage | $100 flat fee |
| Expanded Coverage | Standard Coverage or Homeowner's | 75% of Basic Rate |

**Excess Liability**: If the loan amount exceeds the owner's policy liability, calculate the excess at the difference of the basic rate schedule for the liability amounts and the applicable policy type.

---

## Second Loan - Standard Coverage with Extended Coverage First Loan (Section 205)

When issuing a Standard Coverage loan policy concurrent with an Extended Coverage loan policy insuring a second loan:

- **Calculation**: Increased liability basis at the applicable insurance rates based on the face amount of the second loan
- **Policy Charge**: Plus $100

## High Liability Rate Discount (Section 9)

For policies with liability amounts exceeding $5,000,000:

| Amount of Insurance | Discount |
|---|---|
| $5,000,000 to $10,000,000 | 65% of applicable rate |
| $10,000,001 to $25,000,000 | 60% of applicable rate |
| $25,000,001 to $55,000,000 | 55% of applicable rate |
| $55,000,001 to $75,000,000 | 50% of applicable rate |
| $75,000,001 and above | 45% of applicable rate |

**Note**: This discount applies to the policy premium only, not to endorsements.
